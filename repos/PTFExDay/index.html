About PTF

PTF stands for “Prince Theodore Family”. PTF is a christian weekly gathering for computer science students. We hold our meeting at the cathedral on each Saturday.

OUR VISION

We aim to become the most proactive source of technology that serves our Egyptian christian society including all churches, denominations and communities.

OUR MISSION

Because we believe in the power of the group and that God gathered us for a reason , we invest in our individuals through the following :
  1. Creating a christian spiritual society for our computer science students
  2. Seeking continuous development and improvement of our members on a spiritual, scientific, pragmatic and cultural levels.
  3. Providing technical and advisory support to all our community members.
  4. Discovering and developing the talents of our community members through our committees.
  5. Organizing and Directing the efforts of our members towards their improvement.

WHAT WE DO?

As Computer Scientists in our own right, we had to divide and conquer the management bulk. We’re clustered in several groups , or “Committees” under one central management , called “The high board”. Each committee serves a special function that utilizes the talents of its members to serve our greater goal, our God’s : “Who will have all men to be saved, and to come unto the knowledge of the truth.” (1 Timothy 2:4).
